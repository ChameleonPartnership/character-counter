Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Character Counter is designed as a browser-based text counting tool. This Privacy Policy explains what happens when you use the site.

Text Processing

Your text stays in your browser

Text counting and analysis happens client-side in your web browser. The text you type or paste into Character Counter is not sent to a Character Counter server for counting, storage, or review.

No account is required

Character Counter does not require registration, and we do not ask you to provide your name, email address, or other account details to use the tool.

Browser Storage

localStorage

Character Counter may use your browser's localStorage to remember interface preferences, including theme selection and whether the detailed statistics panel is visible. This information is stored on your device and can be cleared through your browser settings.

Google AdSense

Advertising cookies

If Google AdSense ads are displayed on Character Counter, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. You can learn more from Google's Advertising policies and technologies and the Google Ads policies.

Changes to This Policy

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date shown at the top of this page.
